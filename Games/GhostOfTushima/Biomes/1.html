✦ Traversal & Survival Essentials
  • 風 Follow the Wind AlwaysThe guiding wind changes color and speed in each biome — gold in meadows, pale in mountains, near-invisible in deep forest.
  • 🦊 Chase Every White FoxFox dens are hidden inside sakura groves and bamboo thickets. They only appear once — never respawn.
  • 🌿 Pampas = Free StealthCrouching in tall grass renders Jin invisible to all but Point-Blank enemies. Essential in open meadow camps.
  • ⛰ Upgrade Hook Before KamiagataThe mountain region requires full grappling hook range for cliff shortcuts — upgrade at a Trapper beforehand.
  • 🌧 Rain Muffles FootstepsCedar forest rain events negate enemy audio detection. Time infiltrations during downpours for zero-alarm runs.
  • 🌸 Haiku Spots Need Specific WeatherSeveral Haiku meditation spots are time- or weather-gated — if a prompt doesn't appear, rest until conditions change.

Biome rankings are assessed across four weighted criteria: visual distinctiveness & artistic impact (40%), gameplay utility & stealth potential (30%), exploration density & hidden content (20%), and narrative & lore significance (10%). Tier scores reflect immersive contribution to the island identity of Tsushima.
