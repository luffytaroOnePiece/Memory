✦ Arcane Builder's Grimoire
  • Arcane softcap is 60. Beyond this, returns diminish sharply — invest excess into Vigor or Endurance.
  • Lord of Blood's Exultation (Talisman) grants +20% atk on nearby bleed proc — essential for any hemorrhage build.
  • White Mask helmet also triggers a +10% atk bonus on bleed — stack it with the talisman for near-constant buff.
  • Seppuku Ash of War on an offhand weapon applies a bleed buff and increases the weapon's innate bleed buildup for 60 seconds.
  • Never use pure Arcane scaling weapons with standard Smithing Stones — they require Somber Smithing Stones and cannot be infused.
  • Kindred of Rot's Exultation activates on poison/rot proc — swap for this when running the Venomous Dancer build.
  • The Arcane stat also raises Item Discovery — a hidden benefit making farming easier while you optimize your build.
