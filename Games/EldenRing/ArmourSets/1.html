Best Slot-by-Slot Choices
Head Azur's Glintstone Crown −15% cast speed. Best helm in game if FP drain is managed.
Head alt Snow Witch Hat +10% Cold sorcery dmg. Mandatory for frost INT builds.
Chest Lusat's Robe Best magic defense + Focus of any light chest piece.
Chest alt Carian Knight Armor Best medium chest — 35 poise, excellent phys + magic resist.
Hands Lusat's Manchettes Best magic defense hands in light class.
Legs Lusat's Trousers Solid magic defense. Keep light for fast roll maintenance.
INT Armor Pro Tips
◆Prioritise fast roll above all — staying under 30% equip load is non-negotiable. Sorcerers die from getting hit, not from low armor values.
◆Focus stat matters — higher Focus reduces status buildup from sleep and madness, and improves FP recovery slightly. Prioritise sets with 80+ Focus.
◆Mix and match slots — Azur's Crown + Lusat's Robe + any legs is a common min-max combo. You don't need full matching sets.
◆Erdtree's Favor +2 — increases max equip load by 19%. This single talisman lets many INT builds wear Carian Knight chest while staying fast roll.
◆Going naked is viable — at endgame many glass cannon casters run no armor at all, using the saved equip load budget entirely on weapon and talisman choices.
