💡 Essential Tips

  • ⚡ Stat Priority: Level DEX to 45-50 first, then FAITH to 35-45. Vigor and Endurance to 40.
  • ✨ Ash of War: Equip Lightning-based ashes (Lightning Ram, Thunderbolt) for weapon synergy.
  • 🛡️ Talismans: Use Dragoncrest Greatshield Talisman + Sacred Scorpion Charm combo.
  • 🔮 Spells: Divine Blessing, Golden Lightning Bolt, and Litany of Proper Death create devastating burst.
  • 👑 Role Play: Embrace the paladin fantasy—agile warrior blessed by grace itself.

Ranking Criteria: Based on stat efficiency, scaling multipliers, weapon availability, PvP/PvE versatility, and fashion souls. Rankings reflect meta at patch 1.10 for Elden Ring.
