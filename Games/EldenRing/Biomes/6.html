Leyndell
Royal Capital

The Seat of the Erdtree's Grace
✦

Once the seat of the Elden Lord, Leyndell stands as the most consequential region in all the Lands Between. Gilded spires pierce ashen skies where the colossal arm of a fallen god rests among palatial ruins. No Tarnished may ascend to the Erdtree without first passing through this sacred — and deadly — domain.

01
Defeat Morgott, the Omen King
The true form of Margit awaits at the throne of Leyndell — the mandatory gate to the Erdtree.

Boss Key Item
S+
⚔ Boss Fight
  • Morgott, the Omen King — Mandatory. Extremely aggressive; uses spectral weapons and a spear slam AOE. Summon Melina's shade via the gold summon sign.
  • Equip holy resistance talismans; his later phase adds bleed-style burst combos.
  • The Erdtree will be sealed after this fight — proceed to the Mountaintops of the Giants.
🗝 Key Items Received
  • Morgott's Great Rune — Restores HP with every kill; one of the most powerful runes.
  • Remembrance of the Omen King — Trade for Morgott's Cursed Sword or Regal Omen Bairn.
  • Elden Remembrance — Later tied to the game's final boss; save it.
⚠ Missable Secrets
  • Morgott drops lore dialogue hinting at the Omen's tragic lineage — read item descriptions carefully.
  • If you skip his Margit fight in Stormveil, you miss early foreshadowing and Margit's Shackle (still usable here).

⟡ Exploration Tips for Leyndell Royal Capital
  • ⚖ Recommended Level: 80–110. The capital is a late Altus Plateau area. Below 80, Morgott will hit extremely hard and the outer sentinels will two-shot you.
  • ☁ Dangerous Enemies: Leyndell Knights (aggressive patrol knights with rapid combos), Giant Ants (sewers), Basilisks (Death Blight in the underground), and the mounted knights at the outer gate.
  • 💀 Best Rune Farming Spot: The Avenue Balcony grace → drop down, kill the two giants for ~10,000 runes per run. Easy loop with the grace nearby.
  • 🗺 Map Fragment Location: Found near the Avenue Balcony Site of Grace, on a ruined pillar in the central boulevard. Easy to walk past if you're focusing on the main path.
  • 🔥 Point of No Return Warning: Once you burn the Erdtree (via Fire Giant + Crumbling Farum Azula), Leyndell becomes Ashen Capital — most outer loot and NPCs are permanently changed. Get everything first.
  • 🧱 Hidden Wall in Sewers: In the Shunning-Grounds' deep section, hit the altar / back wall twice to reveal the path leading to Erdtree's Favor +2 and a secret area.
  • 🌿 Things That Lock Quests: Missing the Gold Needle before the Erdtree burns locks Millicent's and Goldmask's endings. Missing Seedbed Curses locks the Fell Curse ending. Missing Fia's Deeproot quest locks her ending.
