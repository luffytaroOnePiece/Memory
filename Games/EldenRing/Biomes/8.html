01
Raya Lucaria Academy
Central Liurnia
The heart of Liurnia — a massive sorcery dungeon teeming with deadly mages. Defeating the boss grants one of three Great Runes and a Key legacy item for Renala's questline.
Boss Dungeon Legendary Loot Key Item
Diff
S+
Main Bosses
  • Red Wolf of Radagon — fast, magical, great for learning dodge timing
  • Rennala, Queen of the Full Moon — two-phase fight; one of the most memorable bosses in the game
Key Rewards
  • Great Rune of the Unborn — enables larval tear respeccing via Rennala
  • Remembrance of the Full Moon Queen — trade for Rennala's spells
  • Academy Glintstone Key — required to enter (found NE of Church of Irith)
Don't Miss
  • Grab the Comet Azur spell from Primeval Sorcerer Azur before leaving Liurnia
  • NPC: Thops — give him a Glintstone Key for reward and his questline
  • The Moongrum, Carian Knight secret encounter before Rennala's fog gate
02
Ranni the Witch Questline
Ranni's Rise, Three Sisters
The longest and most rewarding questline in the game. Unlocks the Age of Stars ending, the Dark Moon Greatsword, and access to Nokron, Eternal City.
Questline Legendary Loot Key Item
Diff
S+
Quest Steps (Liurnia)
  • Meet Ranni at Ranni's Rise in Three Sisters after defeating Glintstone Dragon Smarag
  • Hire all three followers: Blaidd, Iji, Seluvis
  • Descend to Siofra River — speak to Blaidd about Nokron
Legendary Rewards
  • Dark Moon Greatsword — one of the best weapons in the game
  • Fingerslayer Blade — key quest item found in Nokron
  • Age of Stars Ending — unique alternate ending
Missable Warnings
  • ⚠ Do NOT give Seluvis's potion to Nepheli — it locks her quest
  • ⚠ Do NOT progress Volcano Manor before finishing Ranni's quest
  • ⚠ Nokron only opens after defeating Starscourge Radahn in Caelid
03
Caria Manor + Royal Knight Loretta
Northern Liurnia
A haunted manor full of spectral hands and maze-like gardens. Defeating Loretta opens the Three Sisters plateau — essential for Ranni's quest and multiple endings.
Boss Dungeon Questline
Diff
S
Boss Fight
  • Royal Knight Loretta — mounted spectral knight, uses both sorcery and melee attacks
  • Weak to fire and heavy strike damage
  • Reward: Loretta's Greatbow and Ash of War: Loretta's Slash
Inside the Manor
  • Carian Glintstone Staff — excellent early sorcery weapon
  • Ceiling Hand enemies — use torch or fire to reveal hidden passages
  • Sellia Crystal Tunnel shortcut — ladder hidden in manor basement
Post-Loretta
  • Access to Ranni's Rise, Ranni's Rise, and Seluvis's Rise
  • Find Blaidd imprisoned in the Evergaol after Ranni's quest resolves
  • Enter Pidia, Carian Servant in rear manor for rare puppet summons
04
Sellen's Questline & Primeval Sorcerer Azur
Waypoint Ruins / Hermit Village
Meet Sorceress Sellen early, then find her true body and Azur's location for Comet Azur — arguably the strongest sorcery in the base game.
Questline Legendary Loot Secret Area
Diff
A+
Quest Steps
  • Meet Sellen in Waypoint Ruins basement — she sells essential sorceries
  • Find her real body in Witchbane Ruins, Southern Limgrave
  • Get Sellian Sealbreaker from Lusat in Sellia, Town of Sorcery (Caelid)
Key Reward
  • Comet Azur — obtained from Primeval Sorcerer Azur in Hermit Village (Mt. Gelmir area, but questline starts here)
  • Stars of Ruin — from Master Lusat
  • Glintstone Kris dagger from completing her quest
Missable Notes
  • ⚠ Completing the quest has two endings — choose Sellen's side for sorcery rewards or Witch-Hunter Jerren's side for his armor
  • Both outcomes are permanently missable after choosing
05
Glintstone Dragon Smarag
Lake near Academy Gate
A sleeping dragon guards a critical shortcut key. Essential for accessing the Three Sisters region without fighting through Caria Manor first.
Boss Rune Farming Key Item
Diff
A
Combat Notes
  • Smarag is enormous — attack the legs and wait for head slams
  • Extremely resistant to magic — use physical or fire damage
  • Can be sneak-attacked while sleeping for massive early damage
Why Fight It
  • Drops Dragon Heart for Cathedral of Dragon Communion incantations
  • Drops large rune amount — good early farming spot
  • Nearby chest contains the Glintstone Whetblade
Pro Tips
  • You can skip Smarag entirely and grab the nearby key from the Academy Gate Town site of grace area without fighting
  • Fast travel away to reset — great rune farming loop
06
Nokstella, Eternal City Access
Lake of Rot / Ainsel River
Beneath Liurnia lies a ghostly Numen city. Requires Ranni's quest to access via the Deeproot Depths coffin — contains some of the best spirit ash summons.
Dungeon Legendary Loot Secret Area
Diff
A+
What's Down Here
  • Mimic Tear Ashes — arguably best spirit summon in the game (Nokron)
  • Astel, Naturalborn of the Void — boss at the end of Lake of Rot
  • Ranni's questline proceeds through Ainsel River Main
Key Items Found
  • Dark Moon Ring — Ranni quest progression item
  • Nokstella Waterfall Basin grace for Lake of Rot access
  • Fingerslayer Blade — in Nokron, critical quest item
Access Route
  • Coffin in Deeproot Depths (via Siofra Aqueduct) → Lake of Rot → Ainsel River → Nokstella
  • Alternative: Ainsel River Well in Liurnia → Ainsel River → Mini Ranni questline
07
Elden Ring Map Fragment — Liurnia
South, Near Academy Gate
Two map fragments cover Liurnia — grab both early to reveal this massive region and plan efficient routes before wasting time in the fog.
Key Item Secret Area
Diff
A
Fragment Locations
  • South Liurnia fragment — on a stele near the Academy Gate Town site of grace
  • North Liurnia fragment — on a stele near the Liurnia Lake Shore, northeast area
Why Get It First
  • The lake is extremely foggy — without the map you'll wander for hours
  • Reveals hidden dungeon icons and tower locations immediately
  • Both are low difficulty to reach — safe even at Level 1
Nearby Finds
  • Laskyar Ruins — nearby, contains chest with Twinned Armor
  • Church of Irith — free grace, Thops the sorcerer vendor nearby
08
Frenzied Flame Proscription (Secret)
Subterranean Shunning-Grounds
Hidden beneath Leyndell — but accessed via a secret passage in Liurnia. Leads to the Three Fingers and unlocks the Lord of Frenzied Flame ending.
Secret Area Questline Key Item
Diff
A+
How to Access
  • Find the Frenzied Flame Village in far northeast Liurnia
  • Speak to Hyetta (grape questline) — she tracks your path to the Flames
  • Hidden door below Leyndell — strip armor, drop into the well
Warning: Irreversible
  • ⚠ Embracing the Frenzied Flame locks out all other endings
  • Only reversible by completing Miquella's Needle questline (DLC)
  • Hyetta's quest culminates with her becoming a Finger Maiden
Village Highlights
  • Shabriri NPC appears here after some questline steps
  • Flame of Frenzy incantation and Frenzied Burst found nearby
  • Church of Inhibition — invader Festering Fingerprint Vyke awaits
09
Four Belfries & Crumbling Farum Azula Preview
Northwest Liurnia
Four towers each hold an Imbued Stonesword Key slot that teleports you to glimpses of other regions — including a preview of Crumbling Farum Azula and Nokron.
Secret Area Legendary Loot
Diff
A
What Each Belfry Holds
  • Belfry 1 — teleports to Crumbling Farum Azula (preview area)
  • Belfry 2 — teleports to Chapel of Anticipation (tutorial area revisited, get Stormhawk King summon)
  • Belfry 3 — teleports to Nokron, Eternal City (preview)
Key Rewards
  • Stormhawk King Ashes — from Chapel of Anticipation revisit (requires killing the boss you fled at start)
  • Shabiri's Woe — in the Belfry chest
  • Unique environmental storytelling across all three locations
Requirements
  • 3x Imbued Stonesword Keys needed — expensive, buy from merchants
  • One Imbued Key is found at the belfry itself
  • Chapel revisit contains the Grafted Scion — much easier now
10
Albinauric Rise & Rune Arc Farming
Eastern Liurnia Plateau
The eastern cliffs hide an overlooked farming route for Rune Arcs using the Albinaurics near the Palace Approach Ledge-Road site of grace.
Rune Farming Secret Area
Diff
B+
The Farming Route
  • Teleport to Palace Approach Ledge-Road grace (via portal in Liurnia Lake)
  • Use Rotten Stray Ashes or AoE sorcery on the packed Albinauric groups
  • ~50,000 runes per run in under 90 seconds with Golden Scarab talisman
Rune Arc Drops
  • Albinaurics drop Rune Arcs — used to activate Great Rune effects
  • Roughly 1-2 per full route clear
  • Stack with Silver-Pickled Fowl Feet for higher drop rates
Nearby Areas
  • Ordina, Liturgical Town — nearby evergaol puzzle leads to Haligtree
  • Apostate Derelict — find the Omen Bairn key item here
  • Portal to reach the Eastern Plateau is hidden in the Village of the Albinaurics

☽ Exploration Tips for Liurnia
⚔
Recommended Level Range
Enter at Level 40–50 after Stormveil Castle. The Academy requires ~60 to comfortably handle Rennala's second phase. Northern Liurnia (Caria Manor) is best at Level 60–70.
☠
Dangerous Enemy Types
Beware Nox Swordstress & Nox Priest combos in ruins — they stunlock relentlessly. Crystallian enemies in caves are nearly immune to non-pierce damage; use thrusting weapons.
✦
Best Rune Farming Spot
Palace Approach Ledge-Road (Albinauric route) — 40,000–80,000 runes per minute depending on build. Equip Golden Scarab Talisman and Silver Fowl Feet consumables.
🗺
Map Fragment Priority
Grab both Liurnia map fragments immediately on arrival — one near Academy Gate Town, one in the northeast near Liurnia Lake Shore. The fog makes navigation impossible without them.
⚠
Quest-Locking Actions to Avoid
Do NOT give Seluvis's Potion to Nepheli Loux — it permanently breaks her quest. Do NOT prematurely kill Blaidd before Ranni's quest resolves or his invasion reward is lost.
🔑
Easy Entry to Academy
The Academy Glintstone Key is found on a corpse northeast of the Church of Irith — it's guarded only by a sleeping dragon (Smarag) that you can sneak past or lure away.
