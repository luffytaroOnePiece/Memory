🗺 Exploration Tips for Mountaintops

  • ⚔
    Recommended LevelLevel 100–120 before engaging the Fire Giant. Lower levels will struggle with the frost & bleed environment.
  • ☠
    Dangerous EnemiesBorealis the Freezing Fog dragon, Death Rite Birds (night), Giant Skeletons, and Crystallian patrols near Heretical Rise.
  • 💰
    Best Rune FarmingGiants' Gravepost bonfire — the nearby two giants yield ~15,000 runes per run, fast reset with Silver-Pickled Fowl Foot.
  • 🗺
    Map FragmentLocated northeast of the Zamor Ruins site of grace, past the frozen river. Grab it early — the region is vast.
  • ⚠
    Quest-Lock WarningCompleting the Forge of Giants event locks out several questlines. Finish Ranni's, Millicent's, and Latenna's quests before kindling.
  • ❄
    Frost ResistanceEquip Immunizing Horn Charm or stock up on Speckled Hardtear in Physick. Frostbite kills fast in blizzard zones.
  • 🌀
    Secret PortalHeretical Rise is accessed via invisible bridge — walk due east from the tower's front gate along the cliff edge.
  • 🔮
    Latenna's QuestlineBring her to the Apostate Derelict church northwest of the region to complete her arc and receive Erdtree's Favor +2.
