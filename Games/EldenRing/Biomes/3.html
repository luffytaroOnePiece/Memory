Must-Do Things in
Caelid

A blighted, rot-ravaged hellscape born from Radahn's celestial war — home to some of the most punishing encounters and most coveted rewards in the Lands Between. None who enter unprepared survive.

60–80 Rec. Level
18 Key Locations

Scarlet Rot Warning: Stock Preserving Boluses and Immunizing Cured Meat before entering. The rot swamps of southern Caelid will drain your health bar in seconds without resistance.
Completion Checklist
#01
Starscourge Radahn — Festival of Radahn
The defining encounter of Caelid. Battle the gravity-wielding demigod Radahn across the Wailing Dunes in one of Elden Ring's most epic boss fights.
Boss Legendary Loot Questline
S+
▼

Boss Details

  • Starscourge Radahn — Shardbarer demigod at Wailing Dunes
  • Accessed via Redmane Castle Festival
  • Phase 2: he crashes down from the sky
  • Can summon multiple NPCs including Blaidd, Finger Maiden Therolina

Rewards

  • Remembrance of the Starscourge — trade for weapons
  • Radahn's Great Rune — increases all attribute caps
  • Unlocks Nokron, Eternal City meteor impact zone
  • Progression for Ranni, Fia, Dung Eater questlines

Missable Secrets

  • Talk to Jerren at Redmane before and after — triggers Witch-Hunter quest
  • Exhaust Blaidd's dialogue post-festival at Forlorn Hound Evergaol
  • Return to Radahn's arena to find Nokron entrance (shooting star zone)
#02
Sellia, Town of Sorcery — All Seals & Tower
Light all three braziers to open the magical seals blocking Sellia's towers, gaining access to rare sorceries and a critical questline location for Millicent.
Dungeon Secret Area Questline Legendary Loot
S
▼

How to Unlock

  • Climb rooftops and treetops to reach 3 unlit braziers
  • Each brazier opens one of the sealed archways
  • Hidden Sellia Crystal Tunnel entrance in town

Key Rewards Inside

  • Nox Mirrorhelm helmet
  • Lusat's Glintstone Staff — best Int scaling staff
  • Sorcery: Stars of Ruin Legendary spell
  • Flask Talisman (Physick ingredients)

Quest Hooks

  • Church of the Plague — find Millicent for her questline
  • Sellia Hideaway cave entrance (hidden behind illusory wall)
  • Defeat Commander O'Neil in Swamp for Millicent's needle
#03
Farum Greathorn & Greyoll's Dragon Communion
Fell the Elder Dragon Greyoll to gain massive runes and unlock Dragon Communion powers. A legendary farming exploit and essential for dragon-based builds.
Boss Rune Farming Legendary Loot
S
▼

Boss & Farming

  • Elder Dragon Greyoll — passive, attack the tail repeatedly
  • Yields ~74,000 runes (massively boosted by Golden Scarab Talisman)
  • 5 smaller dragons also die, giving bonus Dragon Hearts

Dragon Communion Altar

  • Located at Cathedral of Dragon Communion in Caelid
  • Rotten Breath — essential rot incantation for bosses
  • Ekzykes's Decay — AoE rot flame
  • Dragonmaw / Dragonclaw — physical dragon attacks

Tips

  • Use Hemorrhage (bleed) build to kill Greyoll quickly
  • Greyoll can be respawned each run if using rebirth
  • Cathedral has separate boss: Decaying Ekzykes optional drake

#07
Commander O'Neil — Aeonia Swamp
Rot-rotted commander guarding the heart of the Aeonia Swamp. Defeating him yields a key item for Millicent's questline and a powerful War Cry ash.
Boss Questline Legendary Loot

Boss Details

  • Located in center of Aeonia Swamp — constant Scarlet Rot buildup

#10
Fort Faroth — Draconic Secrets
A deceptively quiet fort hiding one of Caelid's most important items behind a gauntlet of sleeping and roosting bats. Home of Radahn's Great Rune altar.

Key Objectives

🗺 Exploration Tips for Caelid
  • ⚔ Recommended Level: Enter at Rune Level 60–70 for survivability. Radahn himself is best fought at 70–80+. Early exploration (sneak in from Limgrave) is possible at level 30-40 but extremely dangerous.
  • ☠ Deadliest Enemy Types: Miranda Flowers (rot cloud), Grafted Scions, Kindred of Rot swarms, and the Lesser Runebear near Fort Faroth — one of the deadliest overworld enemies.
  • 🌿 Stock Up First: Carry Preserving Boluses (crafted or bought) and Immunizing Cured Meat before the swamp. The Aeonia Swamp center will Scarlet Rot-kill you in under 8 seconds unprotected.
  • 📍 Map Fragment: Found at the Caelid Waypoint Ruins merchant on the western road. Get this first — Caelid is too large to navigate blind.
  • 🔑 Quest-Locking Warnings: Do not progress Commander O'Neil and skip Millicent. Talk to her at Church of Plague before progressing north. Missing this locks out one of the game's most complex and rewarding questlines.
  • 💰 Best Rune Farm: Greyoll bleeding method — equip Golden Scarab Talisman, use Gold-Pickled Fowl Foot, attack Elder Dragon tail with bleed weapon. Nets 250,000+ runes per run with buffs.
  • 🐉 Dragonbarrow Sub-Region: Northern Caelid (Dragonbarrow) has even tougher enemies. The Deathbird and Flying Dragon Greyll bridge boss are notoriously punishing. Reserve for level 80+.
