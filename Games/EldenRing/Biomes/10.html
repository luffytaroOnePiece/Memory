Castle MorneMajor Legacy Site
Storm the besieged castle held by the Misbegotten rebellion and confront its final guardian atop the ramparts.
Boss Legendary Loot Questline
S+
Boss Fights
  • Leonine Misbegotten — drops Grafted Blade Greatsword (Legendary Armament)
  • Multiple Misbegotten Warriors throughout the castle interior
NPC Questline
  • Irina of Morne — Met at the bridge, tasks you with delivering a letter to her father Edgar
  • Edgar the Revenger is found inside the castle; completing this initiates a multi-stage quest with an invasion event
  • Missable: If you skip Irina and clear the castle, the questline locks
Key Loot & Secrets
  • Grafted Blade Greatsword — one of only 9 Legendary Armaments needed for the trophy
  • Whip weapon found in lower rampart storeroom
  • Spiked Palisade Shield early in courtyard area
  • Missable: Graft Blade is unobtainable if you miss the boss

Ailing Village & Raya Lucaria SealEssential Progression
A plague-stricken village hiding a crucial talisman and a survivor whose story unravels the peninsula's dark history.
Questline Legendary Loot Secret Area
S
Key Characters
  • Hyetta — Starts her questline here if she sees a Shabriri Grape; leads to a late-game ending
  • Jar Bairn's story alludes to this village indirectly
Hidden Rewards
  • Raya Lucaria Seal — boosts Glintstone sorceries (found in Raya Lucaria Crystal Tunnel nearby)

Missable Secrets

Isolated Merchant & Map FragmentCritical Exploration
Obtain the Weeping Peninsula map fragment and purchase Bell Bearings unavailable elsewhere for permanent shop access.
Key Item Rune Farming
S
Location Details
  • Map Stele is found on the main road heading south from the Bridge of Sacrifice
  • Isolated Merchant is southwest near the cliff ruins; sells Stonesword Key and Sacrificial Twig
Must-Buy Items
  • Stonesword Keys — extremely valuable throughout the entire game
  • Bubble Shower sorcery — only sold here early game
  • Nomadic Warrior's Cookbook [4] — unlocks important crafting recipes
Missable Notes
  • Map fragment is required for efficient navigation; collect immediately upon entering the region
  • Merchant Bell Bearing is missable — obtained by defeating the merchant (opens his shop permanently at Roundtable)

Earthbore CaveOptional Cave
A rodent-infested cave system ending with a cunning boss that rewards exploration-focused players with a remarkable talisman.
Dungeon Legendary Loot
A
Boss
  • Runebear — drops Spelldrake Talisman (boosts magic negation)
  • One of the hardest optional enemies proportional to the zone level — approach carefully
Loot
  • Spelldrake Talisman — essential for Magic damage resistance builds
  • Various cave moss and crafting materials
  • Golden Rune [3] clusters throughout
Danger

  • Cave entrance can be missed as it blends into the cliff face

Morne TunnelCrystal Mine
A Smithstone-rich mine managed by the Misbegotten. Provides essential upgrade materials for early weapon enhancement.
Dungeon Rune Farming Legendary Loot
A
Boss
  • Scaly Misbegotten — drops Rusted Anchor (great early Strength weapon)
Farm & Loot
  • Smithing Stone [1] & [2] — abundant; use for +3 to +5 weapon upgrades
  • Smithing Stone [3] occasionally found deeper in the mine

  • Rusted Anchor has S Strength scaling at high upgrades; excellent bleed potential

Church of PilgrimageSacred Site
A derelict church offering a Sacred Tear for Flask upgrades, a safe beacon in the mist-drenched south.
Key Item Legendary Loot
B+
Key Rewards
  • Sacred Tear — increases the potency of healing from Sacred Flasks permanently
  • Golden Seed nearby in a glowing Erdtree sapling on the road south
Notes
  • The church serves as a rest point and Sites of Grace is here
  • A Jellyfish Spirit Ashes optional summon can be found near the cliff east of the church
  • Jellyfish Ashes are one of only two summons capable of casting ranged healing — extremely useful

Fourth Church of MarikaSacred Site
A remote church west of the peninsula bearing a Sacred Tear and leading to a secret coastal passage.
Key Item Secret Area
B+
Rewards & Items
  • Sacred Tear — second one available in the peninsula, boosting Flask potency further
  • Wandering Nobles outside drop Runes reliably; good minor farming loop
Hidden Secret
  • A hidden path leads down the western cliffs to Witchbane Ruins where Sellen's true body is imprisoned
  • Critical for the Sellen questline — must visit to progress her story to its conclusion
  • Missable: Players who never check the coastal cliffs frequently miss the entire Sellen sub-quest branch

10
Beside the Crater-Pocked GladeRune Farming Spot
An open field with densely packed sleeping giants and undead crusaders offering the best early rune-per-minute ratio.
Rune Farming
A+
Farming Details
  • Site of Grace nearby allows rapid looping through the sleeping giant cluster
  • ~1,500–2,500 runes per run depending on items used (Silver-Pickled Fowl Foot + Gold Scarab)
  • Sleeping giants award the highest rune value per enemy in the zone
Optimization Tips
  • Use Gold Scarab Talisman (from Abandoned Cave in Caelid) for +20% runes
  • Combine with Silver-Pickled Fowl Foot consumable for brief stacking bonus
  • Most efficient for Levels 20–35 players before moving to Limgrave's later zones

Exploration Tips for Weeping Peninsula
  • ⚡
    Recommended Level Enter at Level 20–35 for comfortable exploration; Castle Morne is best tackled at 30+.
  • ☠
    Dangerous Enemies Runebears in Earthbore Cave are massively overtuned — consider skipping until Level 35+. Demi-humans swarm in packs; use AoE attacks.
  • ◈
    Map Fragment First Grab the map stele immediately upon crossing the Bridge of Sacrifice — it reveals all dungeon icons for the region.
  • ★
    Best Rune Farming Spot Beside the Crater-Pocked Glade: sleeping giants yield ~2,000 runes per loop. Optimal pre-Limgrave endgame levelling.
  • ⚠
    Questline Warning Talk to Irina at the bridge BEFORE entering Castle Morne. Skipping her locks out the entire Edgar / Irina storyline permanently.
  • 🔮
    Sorcery Path Magic-build players must visit Witchbane Ruins west of the Fourth Church to find Sellen's imprisoned body — mandatory for her ending.
  • ⚒
    Upgrade Materials Clear Morne Tunnel completely before buying Smithing Stones — it provides enough Stone [1]–[2] to reach +4 on any weapon for free.
