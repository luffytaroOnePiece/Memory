✦ Biome Exploration Principles
  • IEvery region has a hidden underground layer — look for wells, catacombs, and imp statue barriers to find secret sub-biomes beneath each surface zone.
  • IIThe sky tells the story: golden haze means Erdtree dominion; crimson means Scarlet Rot corruption; eternal storm marks pre-age remnants beyond the current cosmology.
  • IIIStake of Marika placements are breadcrumbs — they mark proximity to significant encounters. When one appears alone in a desolate area, prepare.
  • IVSites of Grace face their light toward the nearest point of interest. Follow the beam — it is the game's quietest navigation system.
  • VInvaders and NPC summon signs are region-gated. Completing hostile NPC questlines before advancing past their home biome ensures you receive their full story arc.
  • VIThe deepest underground zones — Deeproot Depths and the Ainsel River Main — house some of the most critical questline resolutions and are missed by the majority of players.
