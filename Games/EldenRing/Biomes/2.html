Elden Ring Completion Guide

Must-Do Things in Altus Plateau

A grand plateau bathed in the golden light of the Erdtree — a land of crumbling fortresses, formidable demigods, and ancient secrets. Gateway to Leyndell and the culmination of your journey through the Lands Between.

Cure a rotted maiden's affliction. Her fate echoes across four regions of the Lands Between.
Questline Legendary Loot
A+

  • Give Unalloyed Gold Needle to Gowry in Caelid

Exploration Tips for Altus Plateau
  • ⚔
    Recommended Level70–90 before entering Altus Plateau. Engage Morgott at 90–100 for a fair fight without over-leveling.
  • ☠
    Dangerous Enemy TypesWormface enemies near the Forest-Spanning Greatbridge inflict Death Blight — carry Preserving Boluses and keep distance.
  • 🗺
    Map FragmentFound on a corpse near the Altus Highway Junction Site of Grace, north of the Grand Lift of Dectus. Grab it immediately on arrival.
  • ⚡
    Best Rune FarmingThe Windmill Village post-Godskin Apostle offers excellent rune density. Alternatively, sleep-exploit Leyndell soldiers with Sword of St. Trina.
  • ⚠
    Quest-Locking WarningProgressing into Leyndell locks certain NPC questlines. Finish Rya, Tanith, and Nepheli Loux quests before entering the capital's inner ward.
  • 🔮
    Sorcerer BuildsPrioritize Mt. Gelmir and Sellen's questline early. Lusat's staff and Stars of Ruin make sorcery builds dominant for the remainder of the game.
