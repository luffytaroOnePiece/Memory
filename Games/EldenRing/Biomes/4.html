01
Elphael, Brace of the Haligtree
Via Ordina Liturgical Town
The rotted heart of the Haligtree — a crumbling legacy dungeon leading to the most challenging duel in the game. Mandatory for true completion.
 Boss Legendary Loot Dungeon
S+
▸ Expand

Boss Encounters

  • Malenia, Blade of Miquella — Optional but essential; hardest boss in game. Two phases, lifesteal attacks.
  • Erdtree Avatar — Guards the Minor Erdtree in Elphael. Drops Golden Seed + Sacred Tear.
  • Loretta, Knight of the Haligtree — Gatekeeps entry to Elphael itself.

Legendary Rewards

  • Hand of Malenia — Legendary Armament (Malenia's blade)
  • Scarlet Aeonia — Incantation, Malenia's rot bloom dive
  • Rotten Winged Sword Insignia — Legendary talisman, attacks boost damage
  • Malenia's Great Rune — Lifesteal on any successful attack

Missable Secrets

  • Loretta's Mastery sorcery dropped by Loretta — easily missed if rushing
  • Unalloyed Gold Needle must be traded to Gowry BEFORE fighting Malenia
  • Millicent's questline concludes here — summon her or let her die for two distinct rewards

NPC Questlines

  • Millicent — Final questline steps; summon sign near Malenia fog gate. Gives Miquella's Needle or Millicent's Prosthesis depending on choice.
02
Ordina, Liturgical Town
Northern Snowfield Plateau
A ghostly evergaol town puzzle requiring you to light four flames while spectral assassins hunt you. Required to access the Haligtree.
 Key Item Secret Area Dungeon
S
▸ Expand

Puzzle Details

  • Enter the evergaol version of the town via imp statue seal near the entrance
  • Light all four flames on statues across rooftops while Invisible Black Knife Assassins patrol
  • Third flame is hidden on the high rooftop — requires ladder climb near the cliff edge

Enemies & Hazards

  • Invisible Black Knife Assassins — Will one-shot on NG+ if unprepared
  • Death resets all lit flames — use flask and stealth methodically
  • Torrent cannot be used inside the evergaol instance

Key Reward

  • Completing the puzzle unseals the teleporter to Miquella's Haligtree — the only entrance
  • Grab Smithing Stone [8] and various grave consumables from town chests before entering evergaol

04
Astel, Stars of Darkness
Yelough Anix Tunnel → Cave

 Boss Legendary Loot Dungeon

Boss Details

  • Astel, Stars of Darkness — Uses gravity and meteor attacks. Teleports frequently. Extremely aggressive in second half.
  • Has the same HP and moveset as Lake of Rot Astel but an additional dive-bomb meteor barrage

Exclusive Rewards

  • Meteorite of Astel — Legendary sorcery, black hole gravity spell
  • Bastard's Stars — Flail armament with cosmic skill

Access Route

  • Yelough Anix Tunnel is hidden beneath the central snowfield — look for torch-lit hole near cliff
  • Cave of Forlorn (optional) is nearby — yields Flowing Curved Sword
05
Millicent Questline — Final Steps
Near Elphael / Haligtree Sites
The culmination of Millicent's arc begun in Caelid. One of the game's most emotional questlines with branching endings and unique rewards.
 Questline Missable Legendary Loot
A+
▸ Expand

Quest Prerequisites

  • Must have given Unalloyed Gold Needle to Millicent in Altus Plateau (from Gowry)
  • Must have spoken to Millicent at Windmill Village, Mountaintops Grace
  • Speak to her summon sign at the base of Haligtree before entering Elphael

Branching Choices

  • Help Millicent: Defeat her sisters → Receive Miquella's Needle. Use Needle in Dragonlord Placidusax's arena to reverse Frenzied Flame ending.
  • Betray Millicent: Invade and kill her → Receive Millicent's Prosthesis (talisman, boosts dex + attack speed)

Missable Warning

  • Defeating Malenia BEFORE summoning Millicent here locks out her quest ending permanently
  • Miquella's Needle is the ONLY way to reverse the Frenzied Flame Lord ending without starting NG+

Exploration Tips
Survival knowledge before entering the eternal blizzard
  • ⚔️
    Recommended Level
    Enter at level 120 for the Snowfield. Elphael requires 140+. Malenia is viable at 150 — though even level 200 Tarnished weep.
  • 🗺️
    Map Fragment First
    Grab the Map Fragment from the stone pillar south of Apostate Derelict grace immediately — the blizzard obscures everything and you will get lost.
  • ❄️
    Dangerous Enemy Types
    Frozen Albinaurics, Death Rite Birds (night-only), Giant Miranda Flowers, and Putrid Crystalians all inflict Frostbite. Stock Thawfrost Boluses.
  • 💀
    Death Rite Bird Timing
    Death Rite Birds only appear at night. Rest at Site of Grace and advance to nighttime to trigger them — two are found here, both with excellent drops.
  • 📿
    Rune Farming — Best Spot
    Palace Approach Ledge Road grace in Mohgwyn Palace via a Varre questline teleporter is nearby — best rune farm in the game (40M runes/hr endgame).
  • ⚠️
    Quest Lock Warning
    Beating Malenia before summoning Millicent permanently fails her quest. Trade the Unalloyed Gold Needle to Gowry before fighting Malenia.
  • 🔑
    Access Requirements
    Requires both halves of the Haligtree Secret Medallion — Left from Albus hidden in Village of the Albinaurics; Right from Latenna's quest reward.
  • 🌒
    Navigating the Blizzard
    Follow the blue flame torches — they mark the safe path through the central blizzard toward Ordina. Deviate and you'll circle endlessly.
