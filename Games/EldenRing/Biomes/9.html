Nokron, Eternal City

Must-Do Things in
Nokron Eternal City

A sunken metropolis frozen beneath Limgrave, accessible only after felling the Starscourge. Here the Numen dead still wander, ancestral spirits haunt its silver-lit streets, and the bones of a shattered star rest at its core.

Underground Biome Post-Radahn Access Ranni's Questline Mimic Tear Source Lvl 60–80 Recommended

10 Essential Activities
01
Defeat Mimic Tear — The Truest Enemy
The most personal fight in all of Elden Ring: a perfect reflection of yourself, wielding your own weapons and spells. Unequip your gear before the fog wall for a trivially easy victory.
Boss Legendary Loot Questline

Boss Details
  • Mimic Tear — Main boss, copies your loadout exactly
  • Pro tip: Enter with nothing equipped; it spawns naked and helpless
  • Located deep inside the Nokron fog gate, past the silver tears
Rewards & Missable Notes
  • Mimic Tear Ashes — Strongest spirit summon for raw power
  • Larval Tear — Required for respeccing at Rennala
  • Unlocks the path to the Ancestral Woods and Siofra Aqueduct
02
Obtain the Fingerslayer Blade — Ranni's Oath
The central objective of Nokron. Hidden in a chest within the Night's Sacred Ground, this blade is the cornerstone of Ranni the Witch's entire questline and the Age of Stars ending.
Key Item Questline Secret Area
Threat
S+
▼
Questline Critical Path
  • Required: speak with Ranni at her Rise in Liurnia first
  • Located in a chest at the Night's Sacred Ground sub-area
  • Delivers to Ranni; she gives you the Carian Inverted Statue
  • Eventually leads to Nokstella, Astel, and the Moonlight Altar
Area Navigation Tips
  • From Mimic Tear grace, cross the rooftops eastward
  • Watch for Silver Tear ambushes — they mimic objects and urns
  • Avoid the giant rolling Ancestral Follower in the open plaza
03
Night's Cavalry — Crucible Knight Duo
An optional but thrilling gauntlet: two Crucible Knights patrol the Night's Sacred Ground plaza. Defeating them yields one of the rarest ashes of war in the entire game.
Boss Legendary Loot
Threat
S
▼
Combat Strategy
  • Fight in a narrow corridor to prevent both attacking simultaneously
  • Holy damage is their weakness; use Sacred Flame or Litany
  • Kill the spear knight first — its grab attack is the deadliest
Reward
  • Crucible Hornshield Talisman
  • Significant rune payout for mid-game leveling
  • One of the most satisfying fights in the underground biomes
04
Collect All Larval Tears — Respec Currency
Nokron contains the highest concentration of Larval Tears in the game. Multiple enemies drop them, and chests contain them. Essential for experimenting with different builds.
Legendary Loot Rune Farming
Threat
A+

Larval Tear Locations
  • Dropped by Slumbering Wolf (mimics stone wolf)
  • Dropped by Giant Slumbering Wolf near Aqueduct

Usage Note
  • Only Rennala (Raya Lucaria) can respec — costs 1 Larval Tear
  • Total in game is limited; do not waste on trivial respecs
  • Nokron yields roughly 5–7 per full clear of the area
05
Find the Hallowhorn Grounds — Ancestral Spirit
Lighting all bone piles in the underground brings back the Ancestral Spirit boss — a hauntingly beautiful fight against a deer spirit that rewards one of the most unique summons in the game.
Boss Secret Area Legendary Loot
Threat

Puzzle Mechanic
  • Locate and light all 7 spectral deer bone piles scattered around Nokron
  • Once all lit, a summon sign appears at the Hallowhorn Grounds
  • This triggers the Regal Ancestral Spirit boss encounter
Reward & Lore
  • Regal Ancestral Spirit Ashes — majestic summon for exploration builds
  • Significant runes; connects lore to Siofra's Ancestral Followers
  • Beautiful arena — one of the game's most atmospheric fights
06
Reach the Siofra Aqueduct & Enter Deeproot Depths
The path through Nokron connects to the massive Siofra Aqueduct, which in turn gives access to Deeproot Depths — home to the Fia questline, the Lichdragon, and an alternate ending.

Nokron's tilted buildings and broken towers hide illusory walls and hidden passages. Entire sections of the rooftop district are missable if players don't explore every dead-end ledge.

🕯 Exploration Tips for Nokron

  • ⚔ Recommended Level: 60–80. Arrive after defeating Starscourge Radahn in Caelid. The falling star event triggers Nokron's entrance hole in southern Limgrave.
  • ☠ Deadly Enemies: Silver Tears disguise as items and urns. Ancestral Followers have rapid melee combos. Crucible Knights resist most damage types — use Holy or Scarlet Rot.
  • 💰 Best Rune Farm: The open plaza near the Ancestral Woods grace has rapid Silver Tear respawns. With Golden Scarab + Bird Foot, this zone yields 40,000+ runes per run.
  • 🗺 Map Fragment Location: On a raised stone monument near the central open plaza, slightly northeast of the second Site of Grace. Easy to miss due to the overhead camera angle.
  • ⚠ Quest-Locking Warning: Do NOT give the Fingerslayer Blade to anyone other than Ranni. Also: speak to Rogier before entering Nokron — his questline closes if you miss the Stormveil black mark.
  • 🕯 Lighting the Deer Bones: All 7 spectral bone piles must be lit for the Regal Ancestral Spirit. They glow faintly in blue. Check the northeastern ruins cluster — 3 are hidden behind fallen arches.
  • 🌊 Proceed to Deeproot: After defeating the Valiant Gargoyles in the Siofra Aqueduct, a coffin at the waterfall's base transports you to Deeproot Depths. Fia must be at her questline step before this.
