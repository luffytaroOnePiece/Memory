STR Armor Pro Tips
⚔Poise thresholds matter — 51 poise lets you tank a single colossal weapon hit. 79 poise tanks two. 100 (Bull-Goat full) absorbs virtually everything. Plan around these numbers.
⚔Erdtree's Favor +2 is mandatory — adds 19% max equip load. This single talisman is what makes wearing Lionel's or Veteran's with medium roll actually possible.
⚔Great-Jar's Arsenal talisman adds another 19% equip load on top. Running both with 51 END lets you wear Lionel's full set and medium roll comfortably.
⚔Fat roll is viable — don't fear the fat roll on a pure STR build. Bull-Goat at 100 poise means you never get staggered, so your positioning matters far more than your dodge iframe timing.
⚔Endure ash of war supplements poise perfectly — activating it briefly before a boss swing gives a window of near-infinite hyper armor, even at lower poise values.
