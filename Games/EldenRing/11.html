◈ DEX Build Essentials
  • ⚡ Stay under 70% equip load — medium roll is the sweet spot for dex builds. Under 30% unlocks the fastest "quick roll" animation.
  • 🩸 Stack bleed triggering — pair White Mask helm with Lord of Blood's Exultation talisman for a massive dual-proc damage window.
  • 🌙 Night's Cavalry farming tip — rest at the Bellum Church site of grace and ride to bridge. Reset on horseback to avoid long return walks.
  • 🔇 Black Knife silencing — the chest piece nullifies footstep sound. Combine with Concealing Veil talisman for near-perfect stealth invasion setups.
  • 🦅 Raptor + Claw Talisman — the jump attack bonus stacks multiplicatively. This combo is secretly one of the highest burst damage setups in the game.
  • ⚖ Mix & match pieces — don't feel bound to wear full sets. Mixing Raptor chest + Black Knife greaves + Night's Cavalry helm is entirely viable and meta-optimized.
