Essential Faith Talismans
✦Two Fingers Heirloom — Raises FAI by +5. Lets you meet incantation requirements without spending levels.
✦Faithful's Canvas Talisman — Boosts incantation potency by 4%. Stacks with seal-specific buffs for maximum output.
✦Radagon Icon — Shortens incantation cast time. Arguably more useful on FAI than INT builds due to the longer cast animations.
✦Flock's Canvas Talisman — Boosts incantation potency by 8%. The best general damage talisman for pure faith casters.
✦Fire Scorpion Charm — +12% fire damage at cost of -17% physical damage received. Essential glass cannon fire faith builds.
