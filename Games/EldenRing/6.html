Arcane Status Effects Ranked
🩸 Hemorrhage (Bleed)% Max HP burst
Best overall. % HP damage procs instantly, scales with ARC. Works on nearly every boss.
🌿 Scarlet Rot% HP over time
Persistent % HP drain. Best on Dragons and large slow bosses. Ekzykes's Decay covers huge AOE.
❄ Frostbite−20% absorption
Stacking buff. Doesn't deal direct damage but amplifies all follow-up damage by 20% on proc.
☠ Poison / Deadly PoisonHP/s drain
Slow burn. Deadly Poison does 3× the DPS of regular Poison. Best on patient players.
💀 Death BlightInstant kill
Extremely rare, limited bosses. Tibia's Summons can apply it — mostly a niche curiosity.
Essential Arcane Talismans
◈Lord of Blood's Exultation — +20% attack for 20s when blood loss occurs nearby. Near-permanent uptime on any bleed build. The #1 ARC talisman.
◈Millicent's Prosthesis — +5 DEX plus stacking attack boost on consecutive hits. Stacks multiplicatively with RWSI for end-combo burst.
◈Rotten Winged Sword Insignia — Up to +18% attack on successive hits. Mandatory on any fast-hitting ARC weapon build.
◈White Mask (Helm) — +10% attack for 20s on blood loss. The single most impactful piece of gear for any ARC bleed build — not optional.
◈Shard of Alexander — +15% Ash of War damage. Mandatory for Corpse Piler and Bloodboon Ritual builds to maximise each activation.
