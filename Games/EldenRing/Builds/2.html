Essential INT Talismans
✦Graven-Mass Talisman — Boosts sorcery damage by 8%. Best universal INT talisman in the game.
✦Graven School Talisman — 4% boost, stackable with Graven-Mass for combined 12% sorcery buff.
✦Radagon Icon — Reduces sorcery cast time significantly. Mandatory on any serious caster build.
✦Magic Scorpion Charm — +12% magic damage at the cost of -17% physical damage received. Glass cannon essential.
✦Cerulean Amber Medallion — Increases FP pool. Vital for Mind-strapped pure caster builds.
