Essential STR Talismans
⚔Shard of Alexander — Boosts Ash of War damage by 15%. Mandatory on any build using Lion's Claw or Flame of the Redmanes.
⚔Dragoncrest Greatshield Talisman — Reduces all physical damage taken by 20%. The tankiest talisman for STR builds.
⚔Bull-Goat's Talisman — Increases poise significantly. Combined with colossal weapons lets you tank through most boss combos.
⚔Two-Handed Sword Talisman — Boosts two-handed attack damage by 6%. Free damage on any colossal build using two hands.
⚔Erdtree's Favor +2 — Raises HP, stamina and equip load simultaneously. The backbone talisman of every endgame STR build.
