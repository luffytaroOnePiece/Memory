Essential DEX Talismans
◆Rotten Winged Sword Insignia — Boosts attack by up to 18% as you land successive hits. Mandatory for any fast-hitting DEX build.
◆Millicent's Prosthesis — +5 DEX plus stacking attack boost on successive hits. Stacks with RWSI for insane late-combo damage.
◆Lord of Blood's Exultation — +20% attack for 20 seconds when blood loss occurs nearby. Essentially permanent uptime on a bleed DEX build.
◆Claw Talisman — Boosts jump attack damage by 15%. Jump R2 into Unsheathe is the highest single-hit DEX combo.
◆White Mask (Armor) — Not a talisman but +10% attack for 20s on blood loss. The must-have helm for every bleed DEX build.
