  • ✦
    Terra Magicus + Comet Azur Cast Terra Magicus, then stand in the circle and fire Comet Azur — the magic amplification field stacks multiplicatively.
  • ✦
    Cerulean Hidden Tear Mix this into your Wondrous Physick before Comet Azur to eliminate all FP costs for ~15 seconds — enabling an infinite beam.
  • ✦
    Buff Stacking Order Always apply Flame, Grant Me Strength → Golden Vow → then engage. Both are independent multipliers on your output.
  • ✦
    Frostbite → Magic Amplification After frostbite procs, the target takes +20% physical damage for ~30 seconds. Ranni's Dark Moon sets this up perfectly.
  • ✦
    Glintstone Pebble Snapshots Quickly cast Pebble and roll-cancel the animation — you keep the projectile while recovering stamina faster.
  • ✦
    Stars of Ruin in PvP Fire Stars of Ruin from behind cover — projectiles track through obstacles and will home onto rolling opponents.
