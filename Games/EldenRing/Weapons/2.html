⚔ STR Build Essentials
  • Two-Hand for +50% STR Two-handing multiplies your effective STR by 1.5 — a 60 STR build becomes 90 for scaling purposes.
  • Heavy Infusion is King For pure STR, Heavy is almost always the best infusion on colossal weapons — outscaling all alternatives.
  • Poise Threshold: 101 Hit 101 poise to trade through most attacks without being staggered when wearing heavy armor.
  • Stallion + Roar Medallion Equip Roar Medallion + use any roar ash to boost colossal weapon damage by ~10% for the full combo.
  • Soft Cap: 54 STR Diminishing returns accelerate past 54. Two-handing lets you hit STR soft cap effects at just 36 base.
Ranking Criteria: AR ceiling (Heavy +25), Ash of War quality, poise damage, moveset versatility,
PvE & PvP viability, accessibility, and overall fun factor — tested across NG+7 and Colosseum.
