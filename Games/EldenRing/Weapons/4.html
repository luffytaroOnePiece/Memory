⬥ Arcane Build Essentials
  • I Arcane does not boost spell damage except Dragon Communion incantations — do not confuse with Intelligence or Faith when planning caster builds.
  • II The Lord of Blood's Exultation talisman grants +20% attack when Bleed procs nearby — essential for any bleed-centric build.
  • III Occult-infused weapons lose natural affinities but gain pure Arcane scaling — excellent on weapons with high base attack and native bleed build-up.
  • IV Pair White Mask helm with bleed weapons for an additional +10% attack on hemorrhage proc. Stacks with Lord of Blood's Exultation.
  • V Minimum 45 Arcane is recommended for any serious bleed build; 60–80 is the soft cap for most weapon scaling curves.
  • VI Bugged pre-patch: Arcane did not scale bleed buildup. Now confirmed fixed — higher Arcane genuinely increases hemorrhage buildup per hit.
