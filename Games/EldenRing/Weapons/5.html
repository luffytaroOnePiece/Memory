⟡ Faith Build Essentials
  • Soft cap your Faith at 60 for most builds — returns diminish sharply past this threshold.
  • Two-seal casting lets you combine bonuses — hold a Dragon Seal + Godslayer Seal for split Incant types.
  • Sacred Scorpion Charm is your strongest talisman for Holy damage — worth the 10% damage taken penalty.
  • Frenzied Flame Proscription gives massive HP at cost of story choices — worth it on pure survivability runs.
  • Flame, Grant Me Strength + Howl of Shabriri stacked before a boss fight gives ~30% fire damage boost.
  • Haligdrake Talisman +2 is essential if fighting ancient dragons — their Holy resistance is extreme otherwise.
