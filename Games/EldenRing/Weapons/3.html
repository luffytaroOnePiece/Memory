✦ Tarnished's Essential Tips
  • ⚔Soft-cap DEX at 60 for diminishing returns — pair with ARC 45 for bleed builds
  • ⚔White Mask helm + Lord of Blood's Exultation talisman grant +20% ATK on Hemorrhage proc
  • ⚔Shard of Alexander boosts Weapon Art damage by +15% — mandatory for skill-reliant builds
  • ⚔Dual-wielding identical katanas with L1 combos procs bleed in 3–4 hits consistently
  • ⚔Cold infusion on Nagakiba adds Frostbite + Hemorrhage combo — double proc potential
  • ⚔Whetstone Knife unlocks infusions — found in Gatefront Ruins basement
  • ⚔Keen infusion scales purely with DEX. Blood infusion adds ARC scaling + bleed build-up
